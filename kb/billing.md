# Billing & Payments

## Payment Methods

- Credit cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- PayPal
- Bank transfer (Enterprise only, minimum $1,000)
- Wire transfer (Enterprise only, minimum $5,000)

## Billing Cycles

- Monthly plans: Billed on the same date each month
- Annual plans: Billed once per year, 20% discount applied
- Pro-rated charges apply when upgrading mid-cycle
- Pro-rated credits apply when downgrading mid-cycle

## Invoices

- Invoices sent via email within 24 hours of payment
- Download invoices from Settings > Billing > Invoices
- Invoice retention: 7 years for tax purposes

## Taxes

- US customers: Sales tax applied based on state
- EU customers: VAT applied at local rate
- VAT ID can be added in Settings > Billing > Tax Info
- Tax-exempt organizations: Upload exemption certificate

## Failed Payments

- 3 retry attempts over 7 days
- Email notification sent after each failed attempt
- Account downgraded to Basic after 7 days of failed payments
- Data retained for 30 days after downgrade

## Cancellation

- Cancel anytime from Settings > Billing > Cancel Plan
- Access continues until end of current billing period
- No partial refunds for unused time (except within 30-day refund window)
- Data export available for 30 days after cancellation
