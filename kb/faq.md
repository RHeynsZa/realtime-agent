# Frequently Asked Questions

## Account

### How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive a reset link via email within 5 minutes.

### Can I change my email address?
Yes, go to Settings > Account > Email. You'll need to verify the new email address.

### How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Data is permanently deleted after 30 days.

## Billing

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. Enterprise customers can pay by invoice.

### When will I be charged?
Monthly plans are charged on the same date each month. Annual plans are charged once per year.

### Can I switch plans?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

### Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer full refunds within 30 days of purchase. See our refund policy for details.

## Technical

### What browsers are supported?
Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+. Internet Explorer is not supported.

### Is there a file size limit?
Individual files can be up to 5GB. Total storage depends on your plan.

### How do I export my data?
Go to Settings > Data > Export. You can download all your data as a ZIP file.

### What happens if I exceed my storage limit?
You'll receive a warning at 80% and 95% capacity. At 100%, uploads are disabled until you upgrade or free up space.
